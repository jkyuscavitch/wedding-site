2010

August 13th: He saw Her

One Friday afternoon John was working on a friend's car in Dedham. After he had fixed the car, his friend's girlfriend Jamie (the maid of honor) introduced John to her best friend Jess. Their eyes met and it was love at first sight.

John's friend asked him if he wanted to go see a movie with Jamie and Jess, and the rest was history. They went to see Inception together, and in the movie theater they held hands for the first time.

The First Dates

Jess and John saw each other everyday during the first week they met. They spent each night after work going for long walks around Boston.

They walked along the Charles River, around Castle Island and Jamacia Plain Pond, and on the beaches of Nantasket together until the sunset and the lights of the city shined bright. One day during their walk, John asked Jess if she was ready for a relationship. She smiled at him, and so their journey began.

2011 - 2013

The Golden Years

Over the first years of their relationship, Jess and John had a lot of fun together. They spent their time in Jamacia Plain at Jess and Jamie's college apartment, which became the popular place for parties with close friends.After a couple of months dating, they met each other's families, and their relationship began to blossum.

Although there were good times and bad times, throughout those years John and Jess came to realize that the love that they shared was real.

The First Vacations

For those of you who know Jess and John, you know they love to travel. They like going on long drives, blasting their music and singing along to their favorite songs. For their one year anniversary Jess surprised John with a romantic weekend at a beach house in Cape Cod.

Over the years they've done day trips to Maine, New Hampshire and Newport. The funnest trip they had was to Chicago for the Lollapalooza music festival. For their most recent trip to Las Vegas, they rented a convertable camaro and drove down the strip and through the desert. After the wedding, they hope to embark on their first international adventure.

2014

They moved in together

After Jess graduated from Northeastern University, she moved back with her family to be closer to her grandmother. Later that year her grandmother passed away, and her family was devistated.

John showed his support for Jess by spending almost every night with her and her family. It during this time that they realized that they needed the support and comfort that only they could give each other.

They grew closer than ever, and Jess' family grew to love John like a son. Even the Yuscavitch family cat Minnie loved when John came over to play. As the new year began in 2014, they decided to move in together.

August 13th: She said yes!

So how did John pop the question? On their 4 year anniversery, he made reservations at their favorite restaurant Thaiphoon on Boylston St in town. It was a rainy Wednesday night in August, and John was so nervous he dropped his phone in a puddle. All night Jess had a feeling that something was different about him, he was being really romantic, he seemed unphased about his broken phone and he was even wearing a suit!

After their candlelight dinner, John took Jess for a drive around town to all of the places that they walked together the first week they met. From Castle Island, to the Esplinade, they reminisced on the golden years back in college when life was simpler. John took one last long drive all the way to Nantasket Beach.

As they arrived, suddenly the rain stopped and the supermoon shined through the clouds. They got out of the car, John took Jess' hand and got down on one knee. She was so happy and immediately said yes! They went home together and spent the weekend celebrating with John's family in Wells, Maine.
